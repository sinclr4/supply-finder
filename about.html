Andrew Parker's Mission

Andrew Parker, a Bradford City supporter from West Allotment, North Tyneside, started an inspiring grassroots initiative to help people with stomas access emergency supplies when they're out and about.

"I was probably more upset about the thought of having a stoma than being told I had cancer. I thought it would change my life and make things impossible."

Following cancer surgery that required him to have a stoma, Andrew initially felt devastated. However, he soon discovered that reality was very different from his fears. He now lives life to the full - attending football matches, enjoying social outings, and continuing to do everything he's always wanted to do.

The Emergency Kit Initiative

Andrew's positive experience inspired him to help others facing similar situations. He began creating and distributing emergency stoma kits, placing them in public venues across the country. Using items that proved surplus to his requirements, each kit contains three smaller packs with:

  • Sterile wipes
  • A stoma pouch
  • Disposal bags
  • Barrier products

These supplies are enough for "running repairs" if someone is caught without their own supplies - a situation that can be stressful and embarrassing.

Where the Packs Are

As an away supporter, Andrew has personally delivered emergency kits to football grounds including Lincoln City, Leyton Orient, Milton Keynes, and AFC Wimbledon. He's also visited gyms, pubs, and music venues across Tyneside, including The Glasshouse in Gateshead.

Andrew hopes that in the future, major retailers will stock certified emergency packs nationwide, saying: "If that happens, there won't be a problem left for me to solve."

About This Finder

I'm Rob Sinclair, and I was inspired by Andrew's amazing initiative to help the stoma community. While Andrew has been doing incredible work placing these emergency packs in venues across the country, I realized there was a challenge: how do people know where these life-saving supplies are when they need them?

That's why I created the Stoma Pack Finder. This web application helps people quickly locate emergency stoma supplies when they're out and about. Simply enter your postcode, and the finder shows you the nearest locations where Andrew and others have placed emergency kits - whether that's a football ground, gym, pub, or music venue.

The application uses modern web technology to make finding these essential supplies as easy as possible:

  • Real-time postcode search using OpenStreetMap
  • Interactive map showing nearby locations
  • Distance calculations to help you find the closest option
  • Detailed information about each location including opening hours and contact details

My hope is that this tool makes Andrew's generous initiative even more accessible, providing peace of mind to people with stomas and their families when they're away from home. If you know of a venue that stocks emergency stoma supplies, please get in touch so we can add it to our database.

Together, we can help remove barriers and ensure that having a stoma doesn't prevent anyone from living life to the full - just as Andrew has shown us all.
